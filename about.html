Our Mission

TURNING POTENTIAL
INTO POWER

Stackly Sports Club exists to unlock the athlete in every human. We believe sport transforms not just bodies, but character, resilience, and community.

Our mission is to provide every member — from 8-year-olds taking their first cricket lesson to 50-year-old marathoners setting PRs — with world-class coaching, cutting-edge facilities, and an environment where performance is celebrated at every level.

Our Journey

15 YEARS OF MILESTONES

2009

Founded

Stackly opens its doors with a single cricket net, 3 coaches, and a community of 40 founding members driven by a shared passion.

2012

Expansion

Added football, swimming, and basketball facilities. Membership surpassed 200, our first state-level cricket champion crowned.

2015

Sport Science Lab

Launched India's first integrated sport science lab in a private club — VO2 max testing, biomechanics, sports nutrition.

2019

50,000 sq ft Campus

Moved to our purpose-built flagship campus. 12 sport disciplines under one roof. 400+ members. 18 full-time coaches.

2022

Digital Coaching Platform

Launched the Stackly app — live sessions, performance tracking, nutrition logs. Coaching goes beyond the walls of the club.

2026

Today & Beyond

500+ members, 340+ championship wins, 20+ coaches and growing. The mission remains the same — unlock every athlete's potential.
